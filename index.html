Road Roughness Estimation

National Level monitoring & early warning system

Our AI-powered Road Roughness Estimation provides comprehensive monitoring of road quality at national scale using Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) technology and AI algorithms.

Key Features:

  • National Scale Monitoring: Complete coverage of road networks across entire countries
  • Early Warning System: Detect road quality degradation before it becomes critical
  • SAR Technology: Uses SAR Pixel intensity (dB values) for accurate measurements
  • AI-Powered IRI Estimation: Machine learning models for International Roughness Index calculation
  • Continuous Assessment: 24/7 monitoring of road surface conditions
  • Historical Analysis: Track road quality trends over time
  • Maintenance Planning: Data-driven decision making for road maintenance
  • Validation: Ground truth validated with high accuracy

Technology Stack:

  • Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) satellite data
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for pattern recognition
  • Cloud-based processing infrastructure
  • Real-time data streaming and analysis
  • API integration for seamless data sharing

Road Deformation & Subsidence Mapping

Where the ground itself is moving

Our advanced InSAR technology detects millimeter-scale ground deformation and subsidence that could affect road stability, providing early warnings for potential infrastructure failures.

Key Features:

  • High-Resolution InSAR: Millimeter-level deformation detection
  • Vertical Deformation Monitoring: Track ground movement in vertical direction
  • Continuous Coverage: Traditional fleets cannot measure mm-level displacement continuously
  • Early Warning System: Detect sinkholes and subsidence risks early
  • Infrastructure Stability: Monitor bridges, tunnels, and road foundations
  • Time-Series Analysis: Track deformation patterns over time
  • Risk Assessment: Evaluate potential impact on road infrastructure
  • Geological Integration: Combine with geological data for comprehensive analysis

Applications:

  • Bridge and overpass stability monitoring
  • Tunnel deformation detection and safety assessment
  • Landslide risk evaluation for mountain roads
  • Construction impact analysis on surrounding infrastructure
  • Natural disaster response and recovery planning
  • Urban development impact assessment

Lane Marking Degradation

What drivers rely on at night

Using high-resolution optical satellite imagery, our AI analyzes lane marking visibility and deterioration to ensure road safety markings remain effective and compliant with transportation standards.

Key Features:

  • High-Resolution Optical Imagery: Detailed satellite imagery for precise analysis
  • Presence/Absence Classification: Automatic detection of lane marking existence
  • Visibility Scoring: Quantitative assessment of lane marking visibility
  • Automated Compliance Monitoring: Ensure adherence to safety standards
  • Night-Time Visibility Assessment: Evaluate markings under low-light conditions
  • Weather Impact Analysis: Assess marking durability under various weather conditions
  • Maintenance Priority: Identify areas needing immediate attention
  • Historical Tracking: Monitor degradation patterns over time

Safety Benefits:

  • Reduced accident rates through better visibility
  • Compliance with international transportation safety standards
  • Proactive maintenance planning before accidents occur
  • Improved night-time driving conditions and safety
  • Enhanced road safety for all users including pedestrians and cyclists
  • Optimized road marking maintenance budgets

Predictive AI Forecasting for Pavement Degradation

Predict the future of your roads

Our advanced AI algorithms predict pavement deterioration before visible damage occurs, enabling preventive maintenance and extending road lifespan through data-driven decision making.

Key Features:

  • Remaining Useful Life Prediction: Estimate pavement lifespan with high accuracy
  • Future Distress Forecasting: Predict distress levels months or years in advance
  • Resurfacing Timing: Determine optimal timing for road resurfacing
  • Machine Learning Models: Trained on extensive historical pavement data
  • Multi-Factor Analysis: Integrate weather, traffic, and environmental data
  • Cost Optimization: Minimize maintenance costs through optimal scheduling
  • Risk-Based Prioritization: Focus resources on highest-risk areas first
  • Budget Planning Tools: Support long-term infrastructure planning

Economic Benefits:

  • Up to 40% reduction in maintenance costs through preventive strategies
  • Extended pavement life through timely interventions
  • Optimized resource allocation and budget utilization
  • Reduced emergency repair expenses and road closures
  • Improved return on infrastructure investment
